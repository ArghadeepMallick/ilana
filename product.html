know your serum

Mulberries

rich anti oxidants

Mulberries are rich in antioxidants that have the ability to make your skin looking fresh and younger. They help reduce dark spots and remove toxins from the skin thereby reducing acne. They're loaded with hydrating properties that soften the skin and make it appear radiant and glowing.

watch out

for these nasties in other serums

NO mineral oils

A petroleum by-product that coats the skin like plastic, clogging its pores. This is incredibly harmful because it interferes with skin’s ability to eliminate toxins, therefore increasing likelihood of acne and other disorders. One of the concerns with this chemical is the potential for bioaccumulation or accumulation of the ingredient in the body. Although its molecules are typically too large to penetrate the skin, regular use of products with this ingredient can cause the molecules to accumulate in the body due to penetration through broken skin or because of use in lip balms.

NO silicones

Similar to Mineral oil, silicones come with the risk of clogging your pores. We opt for Natural based silicone alternatives such as vegetable glycerin, honey, olive oil esters that give a slip and smooliency to the serum.
